                Greedy policy improvement over V(s) requires model of MDP
                $ \pi'(s) = \operatorname*{arg\,max}\limits_{a \in \mathcal{A}} \mathcal{R}_s^a + \mathcal{P}_{ss'}^aV(s') $

                ùù¥-Greddy Exploration

                • Simplest idea for ensuring continual exploration
                • All m actions are tried with non-zero probability
                • With probability 1-ùù¥ choose the greedy action
                • With probability ùù¥ choose an action at random
                  $ \pi(a|s) = \begin{cases} \epsilon/m +1-\epsilon , & \text{if } a^*= \operatorname*{arg\,max}\limits_{a \in \mathcal{A}} Q(s,a)) \\ \epsilon/m, & \text{otherwise} \end{cases} $

                ùù¥-Greedy Policy Improvement

                Theorem
                For any ùù¥-Greedy Policy ùõë , the ùù¥-Greedy policy ùõë‚Äô with respect to qùõë is an improvement $ v_{\pi'}(s) \geqslant v_\pi(s) $ $ \begin{align*} q_\pi(s, \pi'(s)) &= \sum_{a}\pi'(a|s)q_\pi(s,a) \\ &= \epsilon/m \sum_{a} q_\pi(s,a) + (1-\epsilon) \max_a q_\pi(s,a) \\ &\geqslant \epsilon/m \sum_{a} q_\pi(s,a) + + (1-\epsilon) \sum_{a} \frac{\pi(a|s) - \epsilon/m}{1-\epsilon} q_\pi(s,a) \\ &= \sum_a \pi(a|s) q_\pi(s,a) \\ &= v_\pi(s) \end{align*} $

                Therefore from policy improvement theorem, $ v_{\pi'}(s) \geqslant v_\pi(s) $

                GLIE

                Definition
                Greedy in the Limit with Infinite Exploration(GLIE)

                • All state-action pairs are explored infinitely many times,
                  $ \lim\limits_{k\rightarrow \infty} N_k(s,a)=\infty $

                • The policy converges on a greedy policy,
                  $ \lim\limits_{k\rightarrow \infty} \pi_k(a|s)=1(a=\operatorname*{arg\,max}\limits_{a'} Q_k(s,a')) $

                For example, ùù¥-greedy is GLIE if ùù¥ reduces to zero at $ \epsilon_k = \frac{1}{k}$

                GLIE Monte-Carlo Control

                • Sample kth episode using ùõë: {S1, A1, R2, ‚Ä¶, ST } ‚àº ùõë
                • For each state St and action At in the episode
                  recall Incremental Mean

                $ \begin{align*} N(S_t, A_t) &\leftarrow N(S_t, A_t) + 1 \\ Q(S_t, A_t) &\leftarrow Q(S_t, A_t) + \frac{1}{N(S_t, A_t)} (G_t - Q(S_t, A_t)) \end{align*} $

                • Improve policy based on new action-value function
                  $ \begin{align*} \epsilon &\leftarrow 1/k \\ \pi &\leftarrow \epsilon-greedy(Q) \end{align*} $
